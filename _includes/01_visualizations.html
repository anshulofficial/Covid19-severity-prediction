COVID pandemic severity index (CPSI): this index was designed to help aid the distribution of medical resources to hospitals. It takes on three values (3: High, 2: Medium, 1: Low), indicating the severity of the covid-19 outbreak for a hospital on a certain day. It was calculated in three steps; the index is no longer updated, but you can find more details here and here.
